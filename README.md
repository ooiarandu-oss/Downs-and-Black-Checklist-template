A web-based application developed in R Shiny for conducting methodological quality and risk-of-bias assessments using the Downs & Black Checklist for randomized and non-randomized healthcare intervention studies.

The application automates checklist scoring, calculates section-level and total scores, generates visual risk-of-bias summaries, and exports complete assessment reports in HTML format.

The app is publicly available at: https://ooiaranduresearch.shinyapps.io/downs-black-checklist/

This project is intended for academic and research purposes.

Reference:

Downs SH, Black N. The feasibility of creating a checklist for the assessment of the methodological quality both of randomised and non-randomised studies of health care interventions. Journal of Epidemiology & Community Health. 1998;52(6):377–384. DOI: 10.1136/jech.52.6.377
